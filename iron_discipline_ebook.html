Concept #3: Biological Leverage

Circadian Anchoring

View sunlight within 30 minutes of waking. This sets your cortisol and melatonin timers. Without this, your discipline is fighting your biology. You will lose.

Work for 90 minutes. Stop for 15. Your brain cannot maintain high-frequency firing beyond this window. Respect the cycle or pay in burnout.

The Systemic Day

05:00 AM — No-Input Window

Sunlight + Hydration. No phone. Protect your cognitive silence.

06:30 AM — Deep Work Block I

90 mins on the "One Thing." The task that makes everything else easier.

10:00 AM — Shallow Work / Admin

02:00 PM — Physical Stress

Dim lights. Prep for tomorrow. No blue light allowed.

9 Expert Directives

1. 2-Min Rule
If it takes 2 mins, do it now. Don't add it to the list.
2. Phone Jail
Keep your phone in a separate room during work.
3. Cold Exposure
3 mins cold shower to kill mental resistance.
4. Rule of 3
Only 3 major wins per day. The rest is bonus.
5. Social Fasting
Delete apps. Access social only via desktop browser.
6. Power Naps
20 mins max between 1 PM and 3 PM.
7. Pre-Commitment
Tell a peer your goal. Add a financial penalty.

Process, don't store. Act, Delete, or Schedule.
